Welcome to Rally Team Manager ("we", "our", or "the application"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

3. Advertising

3.1 Google AdMob

Rally Team Manager uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use data to personalize the ads you see.

Data AdMob may collect:

  • Device and advertising identifiers
  • IP address (for approximate location)
  • Device information (model, operating system)
  • Ad performance data

For more information on how Google uses data:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information

When you first launch the app, you will be asked for permission for ad tracking (App Tracking Transparency). You can change this preference at any time in: Settings > Privacy > Tracking

On Android:

You can opt out of personalized advertising in: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
